About Me

Shasha Huang is a Ph.D. candidate in Mechanical Engineering at City University of Hong Kong, specializing in computational modeling of high-entropy materials under the supervision of Professor Shijun ZHAO. Her research focuses on using advanced computational techniques, including molecular dynamics simulations, DFT calculations, and machine learning models to design and optimize advanced materials, particularly for radiation-resistant applications.
